Meet our team

At CBS Personal Finance, we’re a group of passionate and curious students from Copenhagen Business School who believe that financial literacy should be accessible, engaging, and empowering.

We come from diverse academic backgrounds, but share one mission: to make personal finance simple and relevant for all students – whether you’re just starting out or already investing. As members, we work together to organize events, lead workshops, and build a supportive community where everyone can grow their financial confidence.

Marketing

Events

The Events Team at CBS Personal Finance is the driving force behind planning and executing our workshops and events. From reaching out to speakers and coordinating with partners to managing logistics on the day, we ensure every event runs smoothly from start to finish. We focus on creating valuable, engaging experiences that make personal finance approachable and relevant for students. Whether it’s a deep dive into investing or a hands-on budgeting session, the Events Team makes it happen.
